COMPREHENSIVE BENEFITS PACKAGE
========================================

Welcome to TechCorp! We believe in taking care of our employees. This guide outlines all the benefits available to you.

========================================
1. HEALTH AND WELLNESS
========================================

1.1 Medical Insurance
All employees and their dependents (spouse and up to 2 children) are covered under our comprehensive group health insurance plan.

Coverage Details:
- Sum Insured: Rs. 5,00,000 per family per year
- Cashless hospitalization at 5000+ network hospitals
- Pre-hospitalization: 30 days coverage
- Post-hospitalization: 60 days coverage
- Maternity coverage: Rs. 50,000 (after 9 months of continuous service)
- Annual health check-up: Complimentary for all employees
- Ambulance charges: Up to Rs. 2,000 per hospitalization

Exclusions:
- Pre-existing conditions (covered after 2 years)
- Cosmetic procedures
- Dental treatments (unless accident-related)

Claim Process:
1. Inform HR within 24 hours of hospitalization
2. Use cashless facility at network hospitals
3. For reimbursement, submit bills within 30 days

1.2 Accident Insurance
Personal accident insurance coverage of Rs. 10,00,000 provided to all employees at no cost.

Coverage includes:
- Accidental death
- Permanent total disability
- Permanent partial disability
- Temporary total disability

1.3 Life Insurance
Term life insurance of 3x annual salary (minimum Rs. 20,00,000) provided to all employees.

Beneficiary nomination must be completed within first month of joining through HR portal.

1.4 Mental Health Support
Free counseling services available through our Employee Assistance Program (EAP).

Services include:
- 6 free counseling sessions per year
- 24/7 helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX
- Complete confidentiality guaranteed
- Online and in-person sessions available
- Family members can also access (2 sessions per family member)

1.5 Wellness Programs
- Annual health screening (blood tests, ECG, etc.)
- Yoga and meditation classes (twice a week)
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to Rs. 1,500/month
- Sports facilities: Badminton, table tennis available on campus
- Healthy snacks provided in office pantry

========================================
2. FINANCIAL BENEFITS
========================================

2.1 Provident Fund (PF)
Mandatory contribution:
- Employee: 12% of basic salary
- Employer: 12% of basic salary
- Withdrawals allowed for housing, medical emergencies, education

2.2 Gratuity
Eligible after 5 years of continuous service.
Calculation: Last drawn salary × years of service × 15/26

2.3 Performance Bonus
Annual performance bonus based on:
- Individual performance: 70% weightage
- Company performance: 30% weightage

Bonus range:
- Exceeds expectations: 20% of annual salary
- Meets expectations: 15% of annual salary
- Needs improvement: 10% of annual salary
- Below expectations: No bonus

Paid in April each year after annual appraisal.

2.4 Referral Bonus
Refer qualified candidates and earn:
- Junior roles: Rs. 25,000 (after 3 months of joining)
- Mid-level roles: Rs. 50,000 (after 6 months of joining)
- Senior roles: Rs. 1,00,000 (after 6 months of joining)

Paid in two installments: 50% at joining, 50% after completion period.

2.5 Retention Bonus
Long-term retention bonus for employees completing:
- 3 years: Rs. 50,000
- 5 years: Rs. 1,50,000
- 10 years: Rs. 5,00,000

3. WORK-LIFE BALANCE
========================================

3.1 Flexible Work Arrangements
- Work from home: Up to 2 days per week (with manager approval)
- Flexible hours: Core hours 11 AM - 4 PM (arrive between 8-10 AM)
- Compressed work week: Work 9 hours for 4 days, take Friday off (limited slots)

3.2 Sabbatical Leave
After 5 years of service, employees can take:
- Up to 3 months unpaid sabbatical
- For education, travel, or personal projects
- Benefits continue during sabbatical
- Job guaranteed on return

3.3 Parental Benefits
Maternity Leave: 180 days paid leave
Paternity Leave: 10 days paid leave
Adoption Leave: 90 days paid leave
Extended childcare leave: Up to 6 months unpaid (after maternity/paternity leave)

New Parent Benefits:
- Flexible work hours for first 6 months after return
- Nursing room available in office
- Daycare assistance: Rs. 5,000/month for first year
