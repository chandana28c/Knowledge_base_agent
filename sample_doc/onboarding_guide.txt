========================================
BEFORE YOUR FIRST DAY
========================================

Pre-Joining Tasks (Complete within 3 days):

1. Document Submission
   Upload to HR portal (https://hr.techcorp.com):
   - Photo ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
   - Address proof (latest utility bill or rental agreement)
   - Educational certificates (10th, 12th, degree, marksheets)
   - Previous employment relieving letter
   - Previous salary slips (last 3 months)
   - Bank account details (cancelled cheque)
   - PF transfer form (if applicable)
   - Medical fitness certificate

2. Background Verification
   - Respond to verification agency within 24 hours
   - Provide accurate information
   - Completion required before joining

3. System Access Setup
   - Check email for IT setup instructions
   - Create initial passwords (will change on Day 1)
   - Download required apps on personal phone

4. First Day Preparation
   What to bring:
   - Original documents (for verification)
   - Passport-size photos (2 copies)
   - Professional attire (business casual)
   - Notepad and pen
   - Positive attitude!

   What NOT to bring:
   - Relatives or friends (first day)
   - Valuables (jewelry, expensive items)

========================================
WEEK 2-4: BUILDING MOMENTUM
========================================

Week 2: Deepening Knowledge
---------------------------------
- Start taking on real responsibilities
- Increase task complexity gradually
- Attend relevant meetings (not just observe)
- Begin contributing in discussions
- Schedule coffee chats with team members
- Complete department-specific training

Week 3: Gaining Independence
---------------------------------
- Manage tasks with minimal supervision
- Proactively ask questions
- Start participating in team rituals (standups, retrospectives)
- Understand end-to-end processes
- Build relationships across teams

Week 4: Contributing Value
---------------------------------
- Own specific deliverables
- Provide input in meetings
- Identify improvement opportunities
- 30-day check-in with manager
- Provide onboarding feedback

30-Day Milestones:
□ Independently complete assigned tasks
□ Understand team workflow
□ Building relationships with stakeholders
□ Contributing to team discussions
□ No blockers for day-to-day work

========================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
========================================

Q: When will I get my first salary?
A: End of first full month. Pro-rata for partial month in next cycle.

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Through HRMS portal, minimum 3 days advance notice (except emergencies).

Q: Can I work from home during probation?
A: Generally no, unless special circumstances. Possible after 6 months.

Q: How do I access learning resources?
A: LinkedIn Learning, Udemy licenses available through HR portal.

Q: What's the dress code?
A: Business casual. Fridays are casual. Formals for client meetings.

Q: When can I enroll in benefits?
A: Health insurance: Immediate. PF: Automatic. Others: Check with HR.

Q: How do I request equipment?
A: Submit ticket through IT helpdesk portal.

Q: Can I refer someone for a job?
A: Yes! Use referral portal. Earn bonus if candidate joins.

Q: What if I'm running late?
A: Inform manager via Slack/text. Not a problem if occasional.

Q: Who do I contact for payroll questions?
A: Email: payroll@techcorp.com or Ext: 5678
