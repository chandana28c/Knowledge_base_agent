REMOTE WORK POLICY
TechCorp Solutions
Effective: November 2024

========================================
1. OVERVIEW
========================================

TechCorp recognizes the benefits of flexible work arrangements. This policy outlines the guidelines for remote work to ensure productivity, collaboration, and work-life balance.

========================================
2. ELIGIBILITY
========================================

2.1 Who Can Work Remotely?
Remote work is available to:
- All full-time employees after completing 6 months probation
- Employees with satisfactory or better performance ratings
- Roles that can be performed remotely (as determined by manager)

Not eligible:
- Probationary employees (first 6 months)
- Employees on performance improvement plans
- Roles requiring physical presence (facilities, reception, etc.)

2.2 Types of Remote Work

Hybrid Work (Default):
- Work from office 3 days per week (Mon, Tue, Thu mandatory)
- Work from home 2 days per week (Wed, Fri flexible)
- Team days: Entire team must be present on Thursdays

Temporary Remote Work:
- Short-term remote work for specific reasons (medical, relocation, etc.)
- Requires manager approval
- Duration: Up to 3 months

Permanent Remote Work:
- Employees living >50km from office
- Requires director-level approval
- Must visit office at least once per month
- Subject to annual review

========================================
3. WORK HOURS AND AVAILABILITY
========================================

3.1 Core Hours
All remote employees must be available during core hours:
- 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM (India Time)
- Respond to messages within 30 minutes during core hours
- Attend all scheduled meetings via video call

3.2 Working Hours
- Standard 40 hours per week applies
- Log attendance in HRMS portal by 10 AM
- Log out time must be recorded
- Overtime requires pre-approval

3.3 Time Zone Considerations
For employees in different time zones:
- Must have at least 4 hours overlap with India core hours
- Adjust schedule to attend important meetings
- Communicate time zone clearly in email signature

========================================
4. WORKSPACE REQUIREMENTS
========================================

4.1 Home Office Setup
Employees working remotely must have:
- Dedicated workspace (separate from living areas if possible)
- Stable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Backup internet (mobile hotspot for emergencies)
- Quiet environment for meetings
- Adequate lighting for video calls

4.2 Equipment Provided by Company
For permanent remote employees:
- Laptop (standard company issue)
- Monitor (24-inch, on request)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset with noise cancellation
- Laptop bag

One-time work-from-home setup allowance: Rs. 15,000
(For chair, desk, lighting, etc.)

4.3 Employee Responsibilities
- Maintain equipment properly
- Ensure physical security of company assets
- Use equipment only for work purposes
- Return equipment immediately upon resignation

========================================
5. COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES
========================================

5.1 Video Meetings
- Keep camera ON for all meetings
- Use professional background or blur
- Dress appropriately (business casual minimum)
- Mute when not speaking
- Be punctual (join 2 minutes early)

5.2 Status Updates
- Update Slack/Teams status (Available, In Meeting, Away, etc.)
- Daily standup updates in team channel by 10:30 AM
- End-of-day summary in project management tool
- Weekly summary email to manager (Fridays)

5.3 Response Time Expectations
- Urgent messages: Within 15 minutes
- Regular messages: Within 1 hour during core hours
- Emails: Within 4 hours during work hours
- Set out-of-office for lunch breaks >30 minutes

5.4 Communication Tools
Mandatory tools:
- Slack: Instant messaging
- Zoom: Video conferencing
- Jira: Project management
- Google Workspace: Email, docs, calendar
- GitHub: Code collaboration

========================================
6. PRODUCTIVITY AND PERFORMANCE
========================================

6.1 Performance Metrics
Remote work performance measured by:
- Output quality and timeliness
- Meeting attendance and participation
- Communication responsiveness
- Collaboration with team
- Achievement of goals/targets

6.2 Daily Check-ins
Managers may require:
- Morning sync call (15 minutes)
- End-of-day status update
- Weekly one-on-one meetings (30 minutes minimum)

6.3 Performance Review
- Same criteria as in-office employees
- 360-degree feedback includes remote collaboration
- Remote work privilege revoked for poor performance

========================================
7. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
========================================

7.1 Network Security
- Use company VPN for all work activities
- Never connect to public/unsecured WiFi
- Enable firewall on home router
- Use strong WiFi password (WPA3 if available)

7.2 Data Protection
- No company data on personal devices
- Use encrypted cloud storage only (company-approved)
- Lock screen when away from desk (password required)
- Shred printed confidential documents

7.3 Physical Security
- Lock workspace when not present
- No unauthorized persons in workspace during work
- No visible company information in video backgrounds
- Secure all equipment when not in use

7.4 Incident Reporting
Report immediately if:
- Device is lost or stolen
- Security breach suspected
- Unauthorized access attempt
- Equipment damage

Contact: security@techcorp.com or Ext. 9999

========================================
8. EXPENSES AND REIMBURSEMENTS
========================================

8.1 Reimbursable Expenses
Monthly:
- Internet charges: Up to Rs. 1,500/month
- Electricity: Rs. 500/month (permanent remote only)
- Mobile data: Rs. 300/month

8.2 Non-Reimbursable
- Furniture beyond one-time allowance
- Office supplies (pens, paper, etc.)
- Coffee, snacks, meals
- Coworking space membership

8.3 Claim Process
1. Submit expense in HRMS portal by 5th of month
2. Attach bills/invoices
3. Reimbursement in next month's salary

========================================
9. COLLABORATION AND TEAM BUILDING
========================================

9.1 Office Days
All remote employees must:
- Attend office on designated team days (usually Thursdays)
- Participate in quarterly team offsites
- Attend annual company events (kickoffs, celebrations)
- Join important in-person client meetings

Travel to office:
- Advance notice: At least 3 days
- Travel reimbursement: As per company policy
- Accommodation: Provided if distance >100km

9.2 Virtual Team Building
- Monthly virtual team lunch (1 hour, casual)
- Online games and activities
- Virtual coffee breaks (informal 15-min chats)
- Recognition in team meetings

========================================
10. WORK-LIFE BALANCE
========================================

10.1 Right to Disconnect
- No expectation to respond after 7 PM
- Weekends are off (unless emergency)
- Use "Do Not Disturb" status when needed
- Take regular breaks (15 min every 2 hours recommended)

10.2 Avoiding Burnout
- Take full lunch break (minimum 30 minutes)
- Use all allocated leave days
- Separate work and personal space if possible
- Communicate if workload is overwhelming

========================================
11. APPROVAL PROCESS
========================================

11.1 Applying for Remote Work
For hybrid work (default):
- Automatic approval after probation
- Inform team via calendar/Slack

For temporary remote work:
1. Submit request in HRMS (minimum 1 week advance)
2. Manager approval required
3. HR notification automatic

For permanent remote work:
1. Written request with justification
2. Manager recommendation
3. Director approval
4. HR documentation
5. Review every 6 months

11.2 Revoking Remote Work Privilege
Remote work may be revoked if:
- Performance declines
- Security policy violations
- Poor communication/collaboration
- Business needs change
- Employee request

Notice period: 2 weeks (except for serious violations)

========================================
12. HEALTH AND WELLNESS
========================================

12.1 Ergonomics
- Take 5-minute break every hour
- Follow 20-20-20 rule (every 20 min, look 20 feet away for 20 sec)
- Maintain proper posture
- Adjust monitor to eye level

12.2 Mental Health
- Access to EAP counseling services
- Virtual wellness sessions (yoga, meditation)
- Managers check-in on well-being
- Encourage social connection with team

========================================
13. POLICY VIOLATIONS
========================================

Consequences for violations:
1. First violation: Written warning
2. Second violation: Remote work suspended for 1 month
3. Third violation: Permanent revocation of remote work privilege
4. Serious violations (security breach): Disciplinary action up to termination
